Privacy Policy

Namipay recognizes the significance of privacy and data protection. This Privacy Policy describes how we manage personally identifiable information collected from Sub-Merchants who register for our services and provide information to us through the Internet. We may revise this policy as our services evolve, so please check back frequently. If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@namipay.com.sa.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the treatment of personally identifiable information collected from Sub-Merchants who use Namipay payment gateway services, as well as consumer information that we acquire in the course of our business. This Policy also applies to the treatment of personally identifiable information that our back-end technology providers/partners may share with us. This Policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Namipay does not own or control, or to people that Namipay does not employ or manage, or other websites or Internet services that are part of or will be part of the Namipay Network.

Objectives

Namipay aims to:

  • Provide consumers with notice of our information practices.
  • Offer consumers choices as to how their information is used beyond the purpose for which the information was provided.
  • Offer consumers reasonable access to their information and an opportunity to correct inaccuracies.
  • Take reasonable steps to protect the security and integrity of personal information.

Information Security

We work to protect the security of your information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts the information you input. We store information gathered on secure computers. The number of employees involved in the management of the data center that have physical access to these computers is limited. We use advanced security technology to prevent our computers from being accessed by unauthorized persons. It is important for you (Sub-Merchant) to protect against unauthorized access to your Login ID/password and to your computer. Be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer and otherwise protect the password used to access Namipay's services.

Information Collection

Namipay collects personal information on our Sub-Merchants through the registration process, the service agreement that they execute, and various submitted documents of the Sub-Merchant's credibility as required by Namipay from time to time. We may also ask customers to supply personal data when placing an order, such as name and address, telephone number, and email address. Namipay uses this data only for the purpose of filling an order or communicating with the customer or Sub-Merchant about the status of an order. The Sub-Merchants, however, are able to collect personal information on their customers via the purchases that are made.

Namipay may use the data collected in the aggregate to give us demographic information about the entire Namipay audience in order to improve the service to you and to our Sub-Merchants, to perform statistical analysis of the collective characteristics and behavior of our Sub-Merchants and visitors, to measure overall patron demographics and interests regarding specific areas of the Namipay domain and to analyze how and where to use our resources. We use cookies to help us determine which sites users link to, and which services Sub-Merchants and visitors use.

We also track information like the domain name from which you contact us, the pages you request, the products you buy, the referring site, and the time that you spend on the site. For everyone who visits Namipay Sub-Merchant purchase page, we log IP addresses, the type of operating system that your computer uses, and the type of browser software used by you. From the IP address, we can determine the Internet Service Provider and the geographic location of your point of connectivity.

Use Of Information

If you are our Sub-Merchant, then the personal information you provide - name, address, etc. - allows us to inform you about updates or changes to the service and linked to the Namipay website or correct any inaccuracies, please contact us at info@namipay.com.sa. We will make reasonable efforts to comply with your requests.

Namipay may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page, so please check back periodically. Your continued use of our services following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you either through the email address you have provided us, or by placing a prominent notice on our website.

By using Namipay's services, you consent to the collection and use of information by Namipay as specified above. If we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at info@namipay.com.sa.
